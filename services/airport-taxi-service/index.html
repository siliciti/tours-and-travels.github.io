How to Book Airport Taxi

  1. Call/WhatsApp us with pickup location, date, time and flight details
  2. Receive booking confirmation within 5 minutes with driver name and vehicle number
  3. Driver arrives 15–20 minutes before your requested pickup time
  4. Driver waits up to 30 minutes for arrivals after you've landed

Tips for Bangalore Airport Travel

  • Pre-book your cab — KIAL is 45 km from central Bangalore. Peak hours add 60–90 minutes.
  • Leave buffer time — For morning flights (before 8 AM), start 3 hours before departure from most Bangalore locations.
  • KIAL has pre-paid cabs but at significantly higher rates than pre-booked services.
