Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2025

We recommend cancelling at least 24 hours in advance to avoid charges. For urgent changes, call or WhatsApp us immediately.

Cancellation Charges

Cancellation WindowCharge
More than 24 hours before pickupNo cancellation charge — full refund if prepaid
12–24 hours before pickup25% of trip fare
4–12 hours before pickup50% of trip fare
Less than 4 hours before pickup75% of trip fare
No-show (driver has arrived)100% of trip fare — no refund
After trip has commencedFull fare payable — no refund

How to Cancel a Booking

  1. Call us at +919886267815 or WhatsApp +919886267815.
  2. Provide your booking reference number, name and trip date.
  3. You will receive a cancellation confirmation via WhatsApp or SMS.
  4. Refunds (if applicable) are processed within 5–7 business days.

Special Cases

  • Medical emergencies: We consider waiving charges on a case-by-case basis with documentation.
  • Cancellation by us: If we cancel your booking, you receive a full refund plus priority rebooking assistance.
  • Weather/acts of God: No charge applies if the trip cannot proceed due to natural calamities or government-mandated restrictions.
  • Tour packages: Tour package cancellations have separate policies — please refer to your package confirmation document.

Refund Process

Refunds for prepaid bookings are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method. Cash bookings do not qualify for prepaid refunds — any eligible amount will be offset against future bookings.
