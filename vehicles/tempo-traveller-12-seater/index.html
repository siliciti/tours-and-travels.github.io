12-Seater Tempo Traveller Hire in Bangalore — Best for Groups

Planning a group trip from Bangalore? A 12-seater Force Tempo Traveller is the ideal choice for family holidays, corporate outings, school excursions and pilgrimages. Comfortable, spacious, and budget-friendly.

Why Hire a 12-Seater Tempo Traveller?

When your group is between 8 and 12 people, multiple cars become expensive and coordination becomes a nightmare. A single Tempo Traveller with a professional driver keeps everyone together, cuts costs by 40–60%, and makes the journey more enjoyable.

Key Features of Our 12-Seater Tempo Traveller:

  • Pushback seats — Recline and relax on long journeys
  • Roof AC — Powerful air conditioning for the entire cabin
  • USB charging — Charging points at every row
  • Music system — Aux and Bluetooth connectivity
  • Reading lights — Individual overhead lights
  • Large boot — Accommodates luggage for 12 passengers easily
