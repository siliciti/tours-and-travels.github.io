Honda Amaze Cab Rental Bangalore — Feature-Rich Sedan

The Honda Amaze brings European refinement to Bangalore's taxi landscape. With a turbocharged diesel engine and Honda's legendary build quality, the Amaze delivers a premium drive at an affordable price.

Honda Amaze — Key Highlights

The Amaze's 1.5L diesel engine produces excellent torque — making it a great performer on highway stretches to Mysore and Coorg, while remaining smooth in stop-and-go Bangalore traffic.

Features:

  • 1.5L Diesel engine — Best-in-class torque
  • Spacious cabin — Best rear legroom in the compact sedan segment
  • Touchscreen infotainment — Bluetooth and USB connectivity
  • Auto AC — Dual-zone climate control
  • Boot space — 420 litres capacity
