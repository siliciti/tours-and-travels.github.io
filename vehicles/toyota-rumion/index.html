Toyota Rumion — Spacious 6-Seater MUV for Bangalore Travel

The Toyota Rumion is a rebadged Suzuki Ertiga built to Toyota's quality standards — making it one of the most reliable and spacious MUV cabs available for hire in Bangalore.

Why Choose the Toyota Rumion?

When your group needs more space than a sedan but you don't need the full 7-seat capacity of an Innova Crysta, the Rumion is your perfect match. It seats 6 comfortably with captain seats in the middle row.

Features:

  • 6 comfortable seats with ample legroom for all rows
  • Petrol engine — Smooth and fuel-efficient
  • AC with rear vents — Everyone stays cool
  • Large boot — Fits luggage for all 6 passengers
  • USB charging — Front and rear charging points
