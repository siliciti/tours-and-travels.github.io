17-Seater Tempo Traveller Rental Bangalore — Medium-Large Groups

The 17-seater Force Tempo Traveller bridges the gap between the 12-seater and the mini bus. It's the ideal choice for medium-large groups of 13–17 people heading on tours, corporate outings or pilgrimages.

Features of Our 17-Seater Tempo Traveller

  • 17 pushback recliner seats — Comfortable for long journeys
  • Rooftop AC — Powerful cooling for the entire cabin
  • USB charging at every row
  • Tinted windows — Privacy and sun protection
  • Music system — Bluetooth and aux connectivity
  • Luggage compartment — Roof rack + internal space
