Maruti Ertiga — Bangalore's Most Popular 7-Seater Family Taxi

The Maruti Ertiga is India's best-selling MPV and the go-to choice for families who need more than a sedan but prefer an economical option over the Innova Crysta.

Why Ertiga is Perfect for Family Travel

With 7 seats, flexible fold-flat 3rd row, and Suzuki's proven hybrid technology (in some variants), the Ertiga is extremely fuel-efficient for long drives to Coorg or Chikmagalur.

Features:

  • 7 seats (3+2+2 layout) with foldable 3rd row
  • Smart Hybrid technology — Improved fuel efficiency
  • Dual AC — Front and rear vents
  • Large touchscreen — Navigation, Bluetooth, USB
  • Flat-fold 3rd row — Converts to additional luggage space
