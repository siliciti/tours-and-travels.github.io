20-Seater Tempo Traveller Hire Bangalore — Large Groups

The 20-seater Force Tempo Traveller is the go-to vehicle for large family reunions, corporate team outings, school and college excursions, and big pilgrimage groups.

Why the 20-Seater Makes Sense for Large Groups

For groups of 16–20 people, hiring multiple smaller vehicles multiplies your cost and coordination hassle. A single 20-seater Tempo Traveller keeps the group together and saves money.

  • USB charging — Multiple charging points through the cabin
  • Music system with Bluetooth
  • Ample luggage space — Roof rack + rear compartment
