Toyota Etios Taxi in Bangalore — Reliable Sedan for Every Journey

The Toyota Etios is one of Bangalore's most trusted sedans — a well-built, diesel-powered, spacious 4-seater cab that handles everything from airport pickups to long outstation trips with ease.

Why Choose Toyota Etios for Your Bangalore Trip?

With a reliable Toyota diesel engine, the Etios delivers excellent fuel economy and smooth performance whether you're navigating Outer Ring Road traffic or cruising the Mysore Highway.

Key Features:

  • Spacious boot — 400+ litres, fits 2 large suitcases and 2 cabin bags
  • Diesel engine — Efficient and powerful for highway and ghat roads
  • AC — Front and rear vents
  • USB charging — Keep devices charged during your journey
  • Music system — Bluetooth connectivity
